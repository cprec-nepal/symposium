The Challenge

Why This Matters

Despite the critical role pharmacists play in public health, significant gaps remain in their involvement in preventive health practices and national policy management.

There is an urgent need to empower young professionals to lead social change across the health and governance sectors.

Key Objectives

Promote Youth Leadership

Empower students to move into policymaking.

Enhance Advocacy Skills

Educate on antibiotic resistance & medication safety.

Build Research Capacity

Training in Methodology and Data Analysis.
